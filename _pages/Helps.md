<p class="header_box">About Ribocentre-aptamer database</p>
<p>Ribocentre-aptamer is a publicly accessible aptamer dataset and searchable database containing comprehensive information on all APtamers. RNA aptamers are oligonucleotides with high binding affinity and specificity to target molecules, which are expected to become a new generation of therapeutic molecules and targeted delivery materials.</p>
