<h2 class="section-title" id="description">Description</h2></font>
<font>In 2003ï¼ŒLiu and Zhang used the SELEX  methodology to select RNA aptamers against bovine thrombin. These aptamers, T705 and T710, specifically recognize bovine thrombin and inhibit bovine thrombin-catalyzed fibrin clot formation<sup>[<a href="#ref1" style="color:#520049">1</a>]</sup>.<br></font>
<br>
<br>

</div>

<div class="content-section">
<h2 class="section-title" id="SELEX">SELEX</h2>
<p>In 2003, Liu and colleagues used SELEX. Starting from a pool of DNA templates, after transcription, aptamers that bind specifically to bovine thrombin were selected after 7 rounds of selection and amplification<sup>[<a href="#ref1" style="color:#520049">1</a>]</sup>.<br>
Detailed information are accessible on <a href="{{ site.url }}{{ site.baseurl }}/SELEX" target="_blank" style="color:#520049"><b><i>SELEX</i></b></a> page.</p>
